Christmas/Birthday Cards

Choose an individual Christmas card design from our EXCLUSIVE range of 2012 Charity Christmas Cards then decide on the print specification to suit your budget, environmental values or style!

We donate 10 cents from every Christmas card sold from the Christmas Charity Range to Project Futures, help us to beat our previous record of $17,850 for this great cause.

Ordering is easy!

  1. Get started by clicking on our 2012 Charity Christmas Card eBook above.
  2. Choose from one of our standard greetings and font styles (page 4) or create your own. Write your preference for wording and style (e.g Wording 2, Style 1) in the comment box in the quote request form).
  3. Choose your preferred Christmas card. Select your preferred Christmas card design, click the corresponding request a quote link located below your chosen card, specify your desired card quantity, make a note of the greeting and style in the comment box and then hit submit. Before you know it your local Worldwide Centre will be in touch. It's that simple.

 

Don't forget, you can upload your own design, or if you need some professional design help to turn your idea for a card into reality, we're here to help.

Have questions or perhaps can't see what you want? Simply contact your local Worldwide Centre and we'll be sure to help.
