Full colour printing stands out and catches the reader’s eye

Colour brochures & Flyers are the centrepiece of many marketing and sales initiatives. Colour brochure printing can be used to build an image for your company or for numerous other purposes. Colour product images show off your product many times better than black and white or one colour printing. A quality printing design helps distinguish real companies from “here today gone tomorrow” companies.

When it comes to flyer printing, you can’t afford to compromise on quality, yet cost is always a factor. That’s why Print 4 Less doesn’t compromise either. We offer flyer printing services of the highest quality for the lowest possible price. Print 4 Less is your No 1 partner for printing flyers in Australia.

  • Full colour one or both sides
  • Premium quality paper
  • Wide range of standard sizes: A6, DL, A5, stocks, and finishes
  • Standard stock options from: 90gsm Bond or Gloss, 115gsm, 130 gsm,150gsm and 250gsm or 310gsm Gloss or Matt
  • Other sizes, stocks weights and finishes available on request
  • Matt or Gloss Varnish/Laminate available
  • Variety of folding and bundling options
