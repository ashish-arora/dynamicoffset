Offset Printing

Offset Printing requires cost effectiveness and image quality. It excels for multicolour jobs that may require exact spot colour as well as full colour and other special printing effects. It is a commonly used printing technique in which the inked image is transferred from a plate to a rubber blanket, then to the printing surface.

Offset Printing in Sydney tends to be more cost-effective for large-volume print runs play a vital role in the superiority of the printed output.

Offset Printing is also known as four-colour printing although there are many colours in the final output. It’s one of the most common ways of creating printed matter that include newspapers, books, brochures, etc. It’s economical and requires little maintenance. Such printing techniques can be used on wood, leather, plastic, cloth, etc. The image quality is sharp and clean. With offset printing it is possible to use specialty inks and varnishes that are not available for the digital printing process.

Offset Printing provides the following benefits:

  1. With excellent image quality the images are vibrant and crisp
  2. Direct to plate allows the press setup in minutes.
  3. As quantities go up, per piece cost goes down.

The various possibilities of Offset Printing are digital printing, flyers, brochures, newsletters, presentation folders, envelopes, business cards, etc.
